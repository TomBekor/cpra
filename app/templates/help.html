CPRA calculates the frequency of haplotypes without the given antigens in the wanted population.

The antigens input should be in one of the following formats -

1. CSV files with 3 first fields: Sample ID, Antigen, Assignment. Sample ID antigens can be divided into 2 or more files.
Assignment - ‘Positive’ or ‘Negative’ antigen(the negative antigens are optional, and may be ignored).
Can add files of more than one person at a time.

2. Input string(field- Patient positive antigens): id; list of positive antigens separated by ','.

In both formats, the antigens are in 4-digit format.

In addition, select the desired population from the list.
